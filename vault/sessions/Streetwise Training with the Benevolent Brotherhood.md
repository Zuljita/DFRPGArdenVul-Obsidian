# Streetwise Training with the Benevolent Brotherhood

## Summary
[[Vael]] undergoes streetwise training with the [[Benevolent Brotherhood]], leading to the anonymous sale of [[Set Armor]] at [[The Docks]].

## Events
- [[Vael]] contacts the [[Benevolent Brotherhood]] through the [[Baliff's Truncheon]].
- Met "[[Shirley]]", the secretary at their public facade, and left contact information.
- Met "[[Petros]]", who provided a tour of the less savory parts of town and offered pointers on anonymous selling.
- Successfully sold [[Set Armor]] to a contact at [[The Docks]] for 40% of its value, maintaining anonymity.

## NPCs Encountered
- [[Shirley]] (secretary, Benevolent Brotherhood)
- [[Petros]] (trainer, Benevolent Brotherhood)

## Locations Visited
- [[Baliff's Truncheon]]
- [[Arcane Practitioners' Club]] (APC, where Vael received the contact for Petros)
- [[The Docks]]

## Loot / Discoveries
- Sale of [[Set Armor]] (40% value).
- 1 XP for loot.
- 1 XP for exploration.
