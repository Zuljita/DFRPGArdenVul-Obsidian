# Heschius Ban

## Summary
Heschius Ban is an [[Archontean]] god of power.

## First Appearance
Mentioned in the "Announcing DFRPG Arden Vul" document as one of the four major Archontean gods with a temple in [[Gosterwick]].

## Disposition
Described as having "disturbing things" associated with him.

## Known Associates
- [[Archontean Pantheon]]

## Notes
- There is no alignment in GURPS, but implications are that his followers might be morally questionable.
