# Pontifex

## Summary
The Pontifex is the high leader of the [[Cult of Set]] based in the [[Forum of Set]] in [[Arden Vul]]. They are responsible for issuing bounties and directing the cult's activities.

## First Appearance
Mentioned in a bounty letter for the party and [[Susarra]].

## Disposition
Hostile.

## Known Associates
- [[Duty Deacon]]
- [[Cult of Set]]

## Notes
The Pontifex issued a bounty for $2,500 each for the capture or kill of [[Vael]], [[Ioannes]], [[Michael]], [[Uvash]], [[Vallium Halcyon]], and [[Susarra]].
