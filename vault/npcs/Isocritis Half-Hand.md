# Isocritis Half-Hand

## Summary
Isocritis, also known as Isocrates, is an able warrior and a loyal servant of the empire. He is the permanent advisor to the [[Under-Archon]] on tactics.

He co-founded the [[Order of the Subterranean Knights]] with [[Melacorius]], but later had a falling out with him.
