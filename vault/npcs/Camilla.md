# Camilla

## Summary
A favored summoned creature used by [[Ashe Maykum]] — appearing as a primary summoned goat in early delves and later as a summoned bat for reconnaissance. Camilla is repeatedly tasked with carrying gear, scouting ahead, and relaying observations.
