# Cerbactos

## Summary
A "crazy rogue wizard" and archmage, formerly of the [[College of Cinders]].

## First Appearance
Mentioned in a conversation with [[Asrotelion]].

## Notes
- Decades ago, he disappeared from the [[College of Cinders]] along with several priceless tomes.
- He was tracked by [[Asrotelion]] to the ruins of [[Arden Vul]].
