# [[npcs/Hjalti.md|Hjalti]]

## Summary
A captive rescued by the party who was captured by the [[Cult of Set|Settites]] in the [[Great Cavern]]. He was part of a party led by [[Bjorn]] that was searching for the evil sorcerer [[Kerbog Khan]].

## Appears In
- [[sessions/Session 22 - The Oracle of Thoth and The Litany of Light.md|Session 22 - The Oracle of Thoth and The Litany of Light]]

## History
[[Hjalti]] was captured by the [[Cult of Set|Settites]] in the [[Great Cavern]] and thrown into a slave pit. The [[factions/Cult of [[npcs/Set.md|Set]].md|Cult of [[npcs/Set.md|Set]]]] were searching for a "lord of the fungi" and an evil sorcerer named [[Kerbog Khan]], who was wanted by one of the [[College of Cinders|Collegium]]. His party leader, [[Bjorn]], a wizard, died in the fight with the [[factions/Cult of [[npcs/Set.md|Set]].md|Cult of [[npcs/Set.md|Set]]]]. [[Hjalti]] led an escape attempt from the slave pits, but it failed, and he was staked up to die as punishment before being rescued by the party.
