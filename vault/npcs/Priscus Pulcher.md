# [[npcs/Priscus Pulcher.md|Priscus Pulcher]]

## Summary
The leader of the [[Sortians]] faction, who led a rebellion against the established magical order.

## History
He is remembered as a traitor by the Empire. The Sortian rebellion led to a long civil war, and [[npcs/Priscus Pulcher.md|Priscus Pulcher]] was eventually executed by an Exarch.

## Notes
- He was known to have a resemblance to the statues known as "The Philosopher".
- The Sortians created magical statues of him for worship.
