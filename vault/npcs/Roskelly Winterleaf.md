# Roskelly Winterleaf

## Summary
[[Roskelly Winterleaf]] is a [[Halfling]] lieutenant and toll collector operating under [[Phlebotomas Plumthorn]] in the pyramid approaches. He manages checkpoints, demands an "Adventuring License" fee and a tithe from delvers, and keeps Plumthorn’s interest protected with a mix of charm and muscle.

## Roles & Influence
- Lieutenant for [[Phlebotomas Plumthorn]]; oversees halfling toll operations near the pyramid landings.
- Local fixer for small deals and access arrangements; responsive to gifts/favors.

## Relationships
- [[Phlebotomas Plumthorn]]: boss and protector; Roskelly enforces his turf.
- PCs (various): pragmatic rapport built through repeated toll negotiations and information exchange.
