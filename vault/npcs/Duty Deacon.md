# Duty Deacon

## Summary
The Duty Deacon is a functionary within the [[Cult of Set]] at the [[Forum of Set]] in [[Arden Vul]]. They are the initial point of contact for those seeking to claim bounties issued by the [[Pontifex]].

## First Appearance
Mentioned in a bounty letter for the party and [[Susarra]].

## Disposition
Hostile.

## Known Associates
- [[Pontifex]]
- [[Cult of Set]]

## Notes
The Duty Deacon is the one to whom bounty hunters are instructed to show the bounty letter to get in contact with the [[Pontifex]].
