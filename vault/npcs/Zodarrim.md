# Zodarrim

Zodarrim is the sole and central deity in modern Dwarven monotheistic religion.

## Theology
The faith of Zodarrim is a monotheistic religion practiced by dwarves. It appears to be the dominant, if not sole, religious institution for the dwarven people.

## The Clergy of Zodarrim
Several clerics of Zodarrim have been encountered by the party, suggesting an organized priesthood.

- **[[pcs/Uvash Edzuson|Uvash Edzuson]]:** A member of the [[factions/Right for Riches Company|Right for Riches Company]], Uvash is a devout cleric of Zodarrim and has been seen actively attempting to convert others, such as [[npcs/Torunn the Smith|Torunn the Smith]], to his faith.
- **[[npcs/Gedrick Malachite|Gedrick Malachite]]:** A Priest of Zodarrim whom Uvash met in [[locations/Newmarket|Newmarket]]. He warned Uvash to be wary of doppelgangers, a threat connected to the [[lore/War of Dwarves and Doppelgangers|War of Dwarves and Doppelgangers]].
- **[[npcs/Hallsted|Hallsted]]:** A cleric of Zodarrim and a member of the dwarven adventuring party known as the [[factions/Survivors of the Stone|Survivors of the Stone]]. He offered to take a life-stone found by the party back to the dwarven city of **Kazildor**.
