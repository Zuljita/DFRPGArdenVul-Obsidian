# [[npcs/[[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] Maykum.md|[[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] Maykum]]

## Summary
[[npcs/[[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] Maykum.md|[[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] Maykum]], also known as "The GOAT," is an [[npcs/Imperial Goblin.md|Imperial Goblin]] druid and inventor. As a member of the [[factions/Grudge Brigade|Grudge Brigade]] mercenary company, he is frequently hired by the [[factions/Right for Riches Company|Right for Riches Company]] for their expeditions into [[locations/Arden Vul|Arden Vul]], often for an equal share of the treasure.

## Personality and Reputation
[[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] has a well-earned reputation as the "goat-summoning druid." He is a reliable and frequently sought-after mercenary who integrates well with the party's operations, providing both utility through his magic and creative inventions.

## Skills & Inventions
- **Druid Magic:** [[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] is a versatile druid. While he is most famous for summoning goats, he has also been observed summoning bears and bat swarms to aid in combat and exploration.
- **Inventor:** [[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] is the founder of [[Alcheboretal Animossities]], under which he develops and commercializes innovative alchemical and botanical devices. His most notable invention is [[The Living Wheelbarrow]], which he developed as part of his "Shroom for Improvement" prototype project.

## Relationships
- **[[factions/Grudge Brigade|Grudge Brigade]]:** His mercenary company, which serves as the contracting source for his services.
- **[[factions/Right for Riches Company|Right for Riches Company]]:** A recurring employer and his primary adventuring companions. He often works alongside [[npcs/Lacrymosa|Lacrymosa]].
- **[[npcs/Camilla|Camilla]]:** A favored summoned creature that has appeared in the form of a goat or a bat, often used for carrying gear or for reconnaissance.

## Notable Actions
- **First Expedition (Session 1):** [[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] was hired for the party's very first mission into the ruins of [[locations/Arden Vul|Arden Vul]].
- **[[locations/Great Cavern.md|Great Cavern]] Exploration (Session 15):** During an expedition into the [[locations/Great Cavern|Great Cavern]], [[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]]'s summoned bears and bat swarms were instrumental in fighting off local fauna.
- **Construction at the [[locations/Beacon.md|Beacon]] (Session 32):** Alongside [[pcs/Vael Sunshadow|Vael Sunshadow]], [[npcs/Ashe Maykum.md|Ashe Maykum]] helped construct a stone cover over a stairway at the [[locations/Beacon|Beacon]].
