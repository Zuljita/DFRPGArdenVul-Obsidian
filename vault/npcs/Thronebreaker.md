# Thronebreaker

## Summary
Dwarven mercenary fighter (Thrainor “Thronebreaker” Ironvein) who works alongside the party and other delvers in the [[Great Cavern]] region. Noted for brute strength and practical problem‑solving when faced with traps, doors, and heavy obstacles.
