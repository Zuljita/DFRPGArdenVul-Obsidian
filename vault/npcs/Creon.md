# [[npcs/Creon.md|Creon]]

## Summary
[[locations/Gosterwick.md|Gosterwick]] pawnbroker who runs “[[npcs/Creon.md|Creon]]’s Curios,” a reliable source of tips on incoming magic and curiosities. Maintains cordial business ties with delvers, offering early notices and occasional holds on desirable stock.

## Appears In
- [[sessions/Session 2 - Halfling Rent-Seekers.md|Session 2 - Halfling Rent-Seekers]]
- [[sessions/Session 20 - The Outer Caverns of Set.md|Session 20 - The Outer Caverns of Set]]
- [[sessions/Session 28 - Teleport Rugs and Baboons.md|Session 28 - Teleport Rugs and Baboons]]

## Roles & Influence
- Proprietor of [[npcs/Creon.md|Creon]]’s Curios in [[Gosterwick]].
- Information conduit for magical items entering town; maintains lists and notifies preferred buyers.

## Relationships
- PCs (esp. [[Vael Sunshadow]]): recurring customer for arcane supplies and leads.
- [[Rarities Factor]] and other dealers: competitive/adjacent market presence.
