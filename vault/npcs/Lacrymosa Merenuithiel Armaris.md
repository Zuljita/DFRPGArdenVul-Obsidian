# Lacrymosa Merenuithiel Armaris

## Summary
An Elven mercenary archer, frequently hired by the party from the [[Grudge Brigade]]. Known for her combat prowess with a bow.

## First Appearance
[[sessions/Session 4 - Cheese and Crackers and Thoth and Demons.md|Session 4 - Cheese and Crackers and Thoth and Demons]]

## Disposition
Professional mercenary.

## Known Associates
- [[Grudge Brigade]] (employer)
- Party members (frequent associates)

## Notes
Her nickname "Lacrymosa" is derived from her elven grace and deadly accuracy with a bow, evoking a sense of solemn beauty.
Killed [[npcs/Roskelly Winterleaf|Roskelly Winterleaf]] in [[sessions/Session 8a - Never Trust a Scorpion.md|Session 8a - Never Trust a Scorpion]].
