# Ancient Wyrm of the Chasm

## Summary
A terrible, ancient wyrm that is rumored to live at the bottom of the [[Great Chasm]].

## Notes
- This creature is said to be twice as old and twice as potent as the [[Huge Green Dragon]] of the surface ruins, who is rumored to be its daughter.
- It is served by a race of deathless, horned skeletons, possibly the [[Survivors of the Stone]].
