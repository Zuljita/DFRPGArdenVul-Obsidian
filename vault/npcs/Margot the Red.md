# Margot the Red

## Summary
[[Margot the Red]] is a [[Thorcin]] woman and the innkeeper of the [[Yellow Cloak Inn]] in [[Gosterwick]]. She confronted [[pcs/Ioannes Grammatikos Byzantios|Ioannes]] regarding an accidental [[Continual Darkness]] spell cast in his room, demanding a fee for new bedding and reiterating the inn's strict no-spellcasting policy due to potential dangers. Her husband is [[Bricwine]].
