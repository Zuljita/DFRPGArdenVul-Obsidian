# Crallicarus

## Summary

An individual described as an "actor or worse" who is considered "utterly useless" by the author of a found note.

## First Appearance

Mentioned on a blackboard note.

## Notes

- He organizes dramatic readings in the old [[Hopper Coliseum]] near the tomb of [[Marius Tricotor]].
