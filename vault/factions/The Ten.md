# The Ten

## Summary
The ten major gods of the [[Archontean Empire]]'s pantheon, forming the core of the state-sponsored religion.

## Goals
To maintain their divine influence and guide the mortal world according to their respective domains.

## Leadership & Members
The specific members of The Ten are not fully enumerated in the provided text, but they include prominent deities like [[Demma]], [[Heschius Ban]], [[Lucreon]], and [[Mitra]].

## Allies & Rivals
- [[The Seventeen]] (minor gods)
- [[The Twelve]] (formerly sponsored gods)
- Various other pantheons and cults.

## Notes
Temples to all of The Ten can be found in the city of [[Newmarket]].
