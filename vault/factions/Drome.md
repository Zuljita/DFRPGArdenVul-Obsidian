# [[factions/Drome.md|Drome]]

## Summary
The [[factions/Drome.md|Drome]] is a powerful and officially sanctioned organization within the [[Archontean Empire]], responsible for law enforcement and handling sensitive matters. They are known to operate out of the [[House of Sight]] in [[Gosterwick]].

## Activities
- **Investigation of [[npcs/Susarra.md|Susarra]]'s Murder:** The [[factions/Drome.md|Drome]] was officially informed of the murder of [[Susarra]] in [[Gosterwick]]. While they could not officially endorse a retaliatory attack on the [[Cult of Set]], they assisted the party's investigation by providing a blood sample of the victim for magical tracking.

## History
Following the imperial ban on the worship of [[Set]] in 1839 AEP, the [[factions/Drome.md|Drome]] claimed to have successfully eliminated the [[Cult of Set]] everywhere in the Empire.

## Known Sub-factions
- **[[House of Sight]]**: The operational base of the [[factions/Drome.md|Drome]] in [[Gosterwick]].

## Leadership & Members
- [[Audun Yellow-Eyes]]: A logothete of the House of Sight who acted as a liaison with the party.

## Allies & Rivals
- [[Cult of Set]] (historical enemies)

## Notes
The [[factions/Drome.md|Drome]] operates under official sanction, but its members are sometimes willing to provide clandestine assistance to further their goals when they cannot act openly.
