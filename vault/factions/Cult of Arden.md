# Cult of Arden

## Summary
A cult that formed among military officials in [[Arden Vul]], dedicated to venerating the legendary hero [[Arden]].

## Goals
To honor [[Arden]] by attempting to replicate his famous "Twelve Labors."

## Leadership & Members
Composed of military officials from the history of [[Arden Vul]].

## Allies & Rivals
Unknown.

## Notes
- The cult is said to have built its own headquarters within the [[Halls of Arden Vul]].
