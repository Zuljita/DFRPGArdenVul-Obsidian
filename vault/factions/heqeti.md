# Heqeti

## Summary

The Heqeti, also known as "hoppers," are an amphibious race.

## Description
According to the [[Book of Priors]]:
- Most are of low intelligence, but some are smarter and can be mages or demon-anti-priests.
- They are considered "purely evil."
- They were defeated by [[Marius Tricotor]] in 1051 AEP.
- Their fortress, the [[Heqeti Ziggurat]], was conquered, and the survivors retreated into the [[Underearth]].
