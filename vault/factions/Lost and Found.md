# Lost and Found

## Summary
An old adventuring party that used to plunder [[Arden Vul]]. [[Wicktrimmer]] was a member.

## History
The party was led by the wizard [[Balthazar the Blue]] and the paladin [[Grimley]]. They made it as far as the [[Obsidian Gates]] but not beyond. The group disbanded when Balthazar got married and his wife would not let him continue adventuring.
