# Second Chance Company

## Summary
A new adventuring party operating in the [[Arden Vul]] area.

## First Appearance
They were encountered at the [[Muddy Puddle]] tavern in [[Gosterwick]].

## Members
- [[Geleg]]
- [[Tresti]]
- [[Klisko]]

## Notes
