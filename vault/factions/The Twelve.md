# The Twelve

## Summary
A group of twelve gods who are no longer sponsored by the [[Archontean Empire]] and have few remaining worshippers.

## Goals
Unknown, likely focused on regaining influence or pursuing their divine interests without mortal support.

## Leadership & Members
- [[Set]] (worship is illegal)
- [[Horus]] (presumed dead, no longer granting spells)

## Allies & Rivals
- [[The Ten]] and [[The Seventeen]] (the officially sponsored pantheons)

## Notes
PC clerics are discouraged from choosing patrons from The Twelve without special GM permission.
