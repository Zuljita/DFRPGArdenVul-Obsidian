# Seedkeepers

## Summary

The Seedkeepers are a scholarly, more esoteric branch of the [[Priesthood of Demma]].

## Goals

They are focused on guarding ancient agricultural knowledge, sacred fertility rites, and understanding the interplay between magic and the natural world. They often seek to recover lost wisdom.

## Leadership & Members

Scholarly priests, such as [[Ioannes]], are more aligned with this branch than the more common rural clergy.
