# Frigg's Jelly Legs

- **College:** Body Control
- **Type:** Regular; Resisted by HT
- **Cost:** 2
- **Duration:** 1 minute
- **Prerequisite:** Clumsiness

The target's legs become rubbery and unstable. They suffer Bad Footing (-2 to hit, -1 to defend, +1 movement point per hex) for the duration of the spell. If they're already suffering from Bad Footing, the effects are cumulative.
