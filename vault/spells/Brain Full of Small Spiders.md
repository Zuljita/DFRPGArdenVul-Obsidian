# Brain Full of Small Spiders

- **College:** Illusion
- **Type:** Resisted by Will
- **Duration:** 1 minute (Cannot be maintained)
- **Cost:** 3
- **Prerequisites:** Perfect Illusion, Itch

Creates a sensation that spiders are crawling on the subject's head. Causes a -1 penalty to all rolls and halves movement.
