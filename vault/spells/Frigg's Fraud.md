# Frigg's Fraud

- **College:** Illusion
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 2
- **Prerequisite:** Simple Illusion

Causes a magic item to be undetectable by Magery at range. Detecting it with Magery by touch or with spells like Detect Magic requires a Quick Contest with the spell.
