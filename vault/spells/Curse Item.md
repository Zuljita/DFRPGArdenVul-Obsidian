# Curse Item

- **College:** Enchantment
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Cost:** 10
- **Casting time:** 1 hour
- **Prerequisite:** Enchant

Gives an item a minor malevolent effect. The caster and others they specify at the time of casting are unaffected. Magic items resist.
