# Frigg's Elephant Ears

- **College:** Mind Control
- **Type:** Regular; Resisted by Will
- **Cost:** 2
- **Duration:** 10 minutes
- **Prerequisite:** Sense Emotion

Causes the subject's ears to grow to elephant size if they lie. They will droop and flap if they tell additional lies.
