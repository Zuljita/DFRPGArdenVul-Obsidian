- **Duration:**

- **Effect:** Allows the caster to determine the nature and properties of magical items or effects. Can reveal if an item is [[Magelocked]].

## Concepts
### Magelocked
A magical locking mechanism or property that prevents access or use of an item or area by non-magical means. It often requires a specific spell or magical interaction to bypass.
