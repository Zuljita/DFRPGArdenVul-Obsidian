# Chasm Floor

## Summary
The dangerous and often unexplored bottom level of a significant chasm within [[Arden Vul]]. It is likely home to various creatures and holds many secrets, serving as a lower boundary for dungeon exploration.

## Description

A "wild" (mostly not constructed) area within the [[Halls of Arden Vul]], as described in the [[Book of Priors]]. It is connected to the [[Heqeti Ziggurat]].
