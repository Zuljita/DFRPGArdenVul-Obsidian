# Astabelon's Bookstore

## Summary
A bookstore in [[Gosterwick]] owned by [[Astabelon]].

## Wares
It is not a huge bookstore, but carries a variety of simple references, such as:
- Encyclopedias
- Dictionaries (Archontean, Mithric)
- Grammar references
- Basic bestiaries and herbals
- *The Archontean Empire*
- *Dickios and Janius Learn Mithric*
