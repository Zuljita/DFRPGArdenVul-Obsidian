# The Tomb of Ptoh-Ristus

## Summary
Thothian tomb containing the sarcophagus of [[npcs/Ptoh-Ristus.md|Ptoh-Ristus]], accessed via secret doors from areas west of the [[Great Chasm]]. The party encountered animated spearmen and mummies here.

## Connections
- From the [[locations/Pyramid of Thoth.md|Pyramid of Thoth]] through the upper [[locations/Halls of Thoth.md|Halls of Thoth]] (via stairs and passages noted in session 27).
- Adjacent to the west side of the [[locations/Great Chasm.md|Great Chasm]] (reached after flying back across and locating secret doors).

## Hazards & Encounters
- Animated spearman constructs (destroyed)
- Mummies (defeated)

## Notable Finds
- Treasure cache including coins, vases, silver ibis, golden feather, cleric scroll, book; golden death mask of Ptoh‑Ristus; doll‑sized furniture including a tiny magic chair.
