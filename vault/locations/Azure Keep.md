# [[locations/Azure Keep.md|Azure Keep]]

## Summary
The keep built by [[Lady Alexia Basileon]] and the [[Knights of the Azure Shield]] in [[Gosterwick]].

## Description
The walled keep is located in the northeast of the town, by a lake. It was established approximately 20 years ago and served as the anchor for [[locations/Gosterwick.md|Gosterwick]]'s expansion from a small village into a town.
