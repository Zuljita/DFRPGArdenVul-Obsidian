# Halls of Arden Vul

## Description

The Halls of Arden Vul are a massive system of cliffs and caverns. They were discovered by the heroes [[Arden]] and [[Vul the Sorcerer]]. The city of [[Arden Vul]] was later founded above this cavern system.
