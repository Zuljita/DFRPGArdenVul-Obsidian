# Goblintown
## Notes

- **Rumor:** The main entrance to the goblin city is a huge iron gate built into the wall of the [[Great Chasm]]. To reach it, one must find the great stone seat near the [[Sundered Span]] and rappel down from there. Tribute is required for the goblin king to grant entry.
