# Library of Thoth

## Summary
A significant library located in [[Arden Vul]], dedicated to [[Thoth]], the god of knowledge. It contains extensive collections of ancient texts and serves as a center for magical research and historical records. It is also known to contain old books from other religious orders, including "back stacks of the [[Library of Mitra]]".
