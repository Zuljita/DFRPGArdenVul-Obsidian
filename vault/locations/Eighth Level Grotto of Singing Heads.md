# Eighth Level Grotto of Singing Heads

## Summary
A grotto found on the eighth level underground, filled with giant carved heads that sing in recognizable tunes.

## Description
The giant carved heads can sing different tunes. If they sing the right tune, they reveal a treasure vault. If they sing the wrong song, everyone in the grotto dies.

## Level
Eighth level underground in [[Arden Vul]].
