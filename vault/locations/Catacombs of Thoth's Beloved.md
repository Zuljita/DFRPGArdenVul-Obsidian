# Catacombs of [[npcs/Thoth.md|Thoth]]'s Beloved

## Summary
A set of catacombs located just under the [[Pyramid of Thoth]].

## Description
According to a rumor, this is the burial place for the most ardent devotees of [[Thoth]], known as "[[npcs/Thoth.md|Thoth]]'s Beloved." The catacombs are said to be guarded by the power of [[npcs/Thoth.md|Thoth]] himself, which has prevented all intruders from entering.
