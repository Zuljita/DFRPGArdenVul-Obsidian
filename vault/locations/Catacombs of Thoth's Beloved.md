# Catacombs of Thoth's Beloved

## Summary
A set of catacombs located just under the [[Pyramid of Thoth]].

## Description
According to a rumor, this is the burial place for the most ardent devotees of [[Thoth]], known as "Thoth's Beloved." The catacombs are said to be guarded by the power of Thoth himself, which has prevented all intruders from entering.
