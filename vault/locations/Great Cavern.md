# Great Cavern

## Summary
A vast and ancient underground cavern system beneath [[Arden Vul]], characterized by diverse ecosystems, various inhabitants (including goblins, beastmen, and magical creatures), and numerous entrances/exits to other dungeon levels and important locations. It serves as a central hub for exploration and conflicts within the dungeon complex.
