# Boomerang of Returning

## Summary
A magical boomerang that automatically returns to the thrower's hand after being thrown.

## Description
A curved, thrown weapon, typically made of wood, enchanted to fly back to its owner after striking or missing a target.

## Properties
- Magical.
- Returns to the thrower.

## Known History
A highly desirable weapon for a thrown-weapon specialist.
