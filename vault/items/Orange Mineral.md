# Orange Mineral

## Summary
A wondrous orange mineral found in the caverns under [[Arden Vul]], with many arcane uses. Suspected to be [[Arcanum]].

## Description
A wondrous orange mineral found in the caverns under [[Arden Vul]], with many arcane uses.

## Properties
- Arcane uses.
- Signifies a unique source of magical energy if found inside the plateau.

## Known History
The [[city of Arden Vul]] was abandoned when the mines of this mineral ran dry. It is rumored that this mineral can be manipulated by powerful mages for good or ill.

## Notes
The orange rocks are a sign of magical energy.
