# Basileon Signet Ring

## Summary
A signet ring bearing the crest of the [[Basileon family]], found on a noblewoman's corpse.

## Description
A ring typically worn to seal official documents or to denote family lineage. This one features the distinctive emblem of the noble [[Basileon family]].

## Properties
- Bears the [[Basileon family]] crest.
- Indicates connection to the [[Basileon family]].

## Known History
Found on the corpse of a noblewoman, possibly Lady [[npcs/Lady Alexia Basileon|Alexia Basileon]] or a relative.
