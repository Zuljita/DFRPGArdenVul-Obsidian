# Rod of Darkness

## Summary
A magical rod capable of emitting shadows or an area of magical darkness, useful for concealment or hindering vision.

## Description
A slender rod, often made of dark wood or obsidian, enchanted to project an aura of gloom or create localized areas of magical darkness.

## Properties
- Magical.
- Emits darkness.
- Useful for concealment.

## Known History
Found as loot or mentioned in magical contexts.
