# Ring of Free Action

## Summary
A magical ring that protects its wearer from being paralyzed, restrained, or otherwise hindered by magical or physical means.

## Description
A ring, often simple in appearance, that provides continuous magical protection against various forms of immobility, ensuring the wearer can always move freely.

## Properties
- Magical.
- Grants freedom of movement.
- Protects against paralysis, entanglement, etc.

## Known History
A highly sought-after magic item for adventurers. The party possesses one.
