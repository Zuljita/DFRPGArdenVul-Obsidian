# Bag of Holding

## Summary
A common magical item appearing as a normal sack, but possessing an extradimensional space that allows it to hold far more than its apparent size and weight would suggest.

## Description
A seemingly ordinary cloth bag, often made of canvas or leather, but magically enchanted to contain a vast, non-Euclidean interior space.

## Properties
- Magical.
- Extradimensional storage.
- Reduces carried weight.

## Known History
A ubiquitous magic item for adventurers. The party possesses one.
