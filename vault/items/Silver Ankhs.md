# Silver Ankhs

## Summary
Three small ankh-shaped objects made of silver.

## Description
Small, symbolic representations of the ankh, the ancient Egyptian hieroglyphic character that represented the word for "life" and, by extension, life itself. Made of silver.

## Properties
- Three in number.
- Made of silver.
- Ankh-shaped.

## Known History
Found by the party during an exploration.
