# Magical Cauldron

## Summary
A cauldron imbued with magical properties, capable of producing a stew that causes intoxication.

## Description
A pot or kettle, likely made of iron, that is enchanted to produce a specific magical effect when used. In this case, it creates an intoxicating stew.

## Properties
- Magical.
- Produces an intoxicating stew.

## Known History
Found by the party during an exploration.

## Notes
The stew is described as "intoxicating" rather than nourishing, suggesting its primary purpose is recreational or ceremonial.
