# Ring of Water Breathing

## Summary
A magical ring that grants its wearer the ability to breathe underwater.

## Description
A ring enchanted with a continuous magical effect that alters the wearer's physiology to allow for aquatic respiration, enabling them to survive and function indefinitely beneath the surface of water.

## Properties
- Magical.
- Grants [[Breathe Water|water breathing]].

## Known History
A useful magic item for underwater exploration.
