# Wheelbarrow

## Summary
A common, mundane tool used for hauling goods.

## Description
Typically consists of a single wheel, two handles, and a tray for carrying loads.

## Properties
- Mundane.
- Useful for transporting heavy items over terrain.

## Known History
[[pcs/Vael Sunshadow|Vael]] used one to push his pack up the [[Long Stair]] to [[Arden Vul]].

## Notes
Can be enchanted to become a [[Living Wheelbarrow]].
