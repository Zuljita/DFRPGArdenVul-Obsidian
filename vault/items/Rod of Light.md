# Rod of Light

## Summary
A magical rod capable of emitting bright light, illuminating dark areas, and potentially harming creatures sensitive to light.

## Description
A slender rod, often made of polished wood or metal, enchanted to project powerful beams or a constant aura of light.

## Properties
- Magical.
- Emits light.
- Potentially harmful to certain creatures.

## Known History
Found as loot or mentioned in magical contexts.
