# Bronze Brooch

## Summary
A small decorative clasp, often used to fasten cloaks or other garments. One such brooch was found by the party.

## Description
A small decorative clasp, often used to fasten cloaks or other garments.

## Properties
- Ioannes' personal attire includes a bronze brooch fastening his red woolen cloak.

## Known History
- Used by high-ranking priests of [[Demma]] as a ceremonial fastener for their hooded cloaks, often in the shape of a seed.
