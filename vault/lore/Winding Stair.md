# Winding Stair Details

This document outlines details regarding the physical characteristics and travel times of the [[Long Stair|cliff face]] leading to [[Arden Vul]].

## Travel Times & Features

-   Walking up the [[Long Stair|cliff face]] takes about 90 minutes, depending on caution and encumbrance.
-   [[Levitation|Levitating]] takes about 10 minutes.
-   Flight should get there in 3 minutes.

## Notable Features on the Ascent

-   There is a [[Cave with Fire Pit|cave with a fire pit]] about halfway up the [[Long Stair|cliff face]].
-   Another [[Cave with Spider Webs|cave with spider webs]] is located over the next switchback.

## Dimensions

-   The [[Long Stair|cliff]] is 1500' high, or 500 hexes.
