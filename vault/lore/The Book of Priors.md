# The Book of Priors

6 volumes, in Mithric

Written by a succession of [[Thothian]] priors, started by [[Gaius Probus]] in 937 AEP,
continuing until 1782 AEP.

## History:
- Discovery of the site by [[Arden]] and [[Vul the Sorcerer]] (829 AEP),
- Founding of [[Arden Vul|city of Arden Vul]] (893 AEP),
- Building the [[Great Pyramid of Thoth]], the [[Halls of Thoth]], the [[Well of Light]], etc.,
- Discovery of [[The Great Chasm]] and [[The Obsidian Gates]],
- Wars against the [[Varumani]] (trolls), defeated by [[Marius Tricotor]] (1043 AEP),
- Wars against the the [[heqeti]] (hoppers), defeated by [[Marius Tricotor]] (1051 AEP),
- Breaking of the [[Compact of Ennius]] with the [[Dragons]] (1588 AEP),
- Disputes between the old temples and the [[Sortians]], leading to the [[Civil War]] (1698 AEP),

## Architecture:
The [[Halls of Arden Vul]] are about 12 levels deep,
- [[Pyramid of Thoth]] (tall, but not many rooms inside)
- [[Hall of Heroes]] (small)
- [[Well of Light]]
- [[Halls of Thoth]] (Upper Great Hall of Thoth is within this)
- [[Forum of Set]]
- [[Obsidian Gates]]
- [[Lower Halls of the Archons]]
- [[The Canyon]]
- [[Lower Shrines and Tombs]]
- [[Troll Thegn]] (mostly [[Varumani]] rather than the Halls proper)
- [[Lower Temple]]
- [[Chasm Floor]] (mostly wild rather than constructed)
- [[Heqeti Ziggurat]] (connected to the [[Chasm Floor]])

## Varumani:
- Big, strong, smart,
- Regenerate; can only be killed with fire or acid,
- Some mages,
- Eventually surrendered to [[Marius Tricotor]] and became loyal Imperial subjects,

## Heqeti:
- Amphibian,
- Several types, with most being the less intelligent "hoppers" but some smarter.
- Some mages and demon-anti-priests,
- Purely evil,
- Their [[Heqeti Ziggurat]] was conquered by [[Marius Tricotor]], but survivors retreated into the [[Underearth]]
