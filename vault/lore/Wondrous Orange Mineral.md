# Wondrous Orange Mineral

## Summary
A wondrous orange mineral that was once mined in the caverns under [[Arden Vul]].

## Lore
- **Rumor 1:** The caverns produced a large supply of this mineral, which had many arcane uses. The city was abandoned when the mines ran dry. ^rumor1
- **Rumor 2:** A unique source of magical energy exists inside the plateau, which can be manipulated by powerful mages. This rumor advises to "look for the orange rocks." ^rumor2
