# Factors Overview

## Summary of Merchant Factors in Gosterwick

This document compiles information about the various merchant "Factors" operating in and around [[Gosterwick]], including their characteristics and services.

### [[Rarities Factor]]
- Led by local goblin [[Wicktrimmer]].
- Offers the lowest storage fee in [[Gosterwick]]; now free for the party due to a deal for [[Larel's Dagger]].
- Willing to house non-cash goods.
- Has locations only in [[Narsileon]], [[Newmarket]], and [[Gosterwick]].

### [[Prosperity Factor]]
- An Empire-wide factor.
- Offers medium storage fees in [[Gosterwick]].
- Three statue brokers working at the [[Inn of the Broken Head]] represent the [[Prosperity Factor]], [[Golden Band]], and [[Wisdom Factor]].

### [[Silent Factor]]
- An Empire-wide factor.
- Known for dressing in black with veils and using a kind of sign language with interpreters.
- Commands the highest storage fee in [[Gosterwick]], but offers the highest security and most privacy.

### Other Factors Mentioned

#### [[Golden Band]]
- An Empire-wide factor.
- Represented by one of the statue brokers at the [[Inn of the Broken Head]].

#### [[Wisdom Factor]]
- A merchant factor.
- Represented by one of the statue brokers at the [[Inn of the Broken Head]].

## Locations of Factors

- **[[Gosterwick]]**: Has branches of [[Rarities Factor]], [[Prosperity Factor]], and [[Silent Factor]].
- **[[Newmarket]]**: Has all five named Factors ([[Rarities Factor]], [[Prosperity Factor]], [[Silent Factor]], [[Golden Band]], [[Wisdom Factor]]).
- **[[Narsileon]]**: Has more Factors than [[Newmarket]].
