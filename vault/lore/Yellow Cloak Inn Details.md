# [[locations/Yellow Cloak Inn.md|Yellow Cloak Inn]] Details

This document provides a detailed overview of the [[Yellow Cloak Inn]] in [[Gosterwick]].

## Overview

The [[Yellow Cloak Inn]] is a prominent establishment in [[Gosterwick]], serving as a common lodging for adventurers. It is a 5-story building near the [[Central Market]], with a 3-story addition and stables in the back.

## Layout and Services

The inn's interior includes:
- A reception area
- A lounge
- An upper-class common room (offering better, but more expensive, food)
- A main common room
- Various individual guest rooms (maximum occupancy 3)
- Dormitories with bunkbeds

A standard $150/week cost of living covers a room and three meals per day.

## Staff

The inn is run by:
- **[[Margot the Red]]**: The innkeeper, a [[Thorcin]] woman.
- **[[Bricwine]]**: [[Margot the Red|Margot's]] husband, who serves as the upper-class bartender.
- **[[Helle]]**: A [[Wiskin]] woman, who tends bar in the main common room.

## Rules and Observations

- The inn staff dislikes people casting spells on the premises, especially if critical failures occur.
- While once a frequent residence for party members, [[pcs/Vael Sunshadow|Vael]] and [[pcs/Ioannes Grammatikos Byzantios|Ioannes]] now stay in the [[Arcane Practitioners' Club]].
