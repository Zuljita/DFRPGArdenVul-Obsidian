# Stirge Fever

## Summary
A debilitating disease or affliction transmitted by stirges, known for its draining effects.

## Description
An illness contracted through the bite or feeding of stirges. Symptoms likely include weakness, fatigue, and possibly a fever, stemming from the blood loss and potential infection.

## Related Entities
- Stirges (primary vector)

## Notes
Can severely weaken individuals if not treated, making them more vulnerable.
