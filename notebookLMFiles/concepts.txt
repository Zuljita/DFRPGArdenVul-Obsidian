# Stirge Fever

## Summary
A debilitating disease or affliction transmitted by [[npcs/Stirges.md|stirges]], known for its draining effects.

## Description
An illness contracted through the bite or feeding of [[npcs/Stirges.md|stirges]]. Symptoms likely include weakness, fatigue, and possibly a fever, stemming from the blood loss and potential infection.

## Related Entities
- [[npcs/Stirges.md|Stirges]] (primary vector)

## Notes
Can severely weaken individuals if not treated, making them more vulnerable.
